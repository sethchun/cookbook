Sunday Gravy

Ingredients:

  • 1 Brown/Yellow Onion
  • 5-6 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Carrot
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Wine (Pinot Noir or Chianti pairs well)
  • 1-2 lbs. Bone-in Meat (Beef or Pork)
  • 1 lbs. Italian Sausages
  • 2 - 28oz. Cans Whole Peeled Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Herbs/Seasonings:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Fresh Basil

Instructions:

  1. Prep onions, garlic, and carrot.
    • Dice onion.
    • You can mince or slice garlic cloves. I prefer slicing thinly (think Goodfellas, but doesn't have to be "razor thin")
    • Peel and slice carrot into ⅓'s or ¼'s
  2. Heat a heavy bottomed pot to medium-high heat and brown meat using olive oil. Set aside.
  3. Add a little more olive oil and saute onions until transluscent. Add garlic and saute until fragrant (30 sec to 1 min).
  4. Deglaze with red wine, scraping fond off bottom of pan. Cook off alcohol (~5 min). Onions and garlic will look almost gelatenous with wine once ready.
  5. Add tomato paste and continue to cook (2 - 3 min)
  6. Add canned tomatoes and mix well. Then add browned meat to pot making sure that all meat is covered.
  7. Add salt, pepper, carrots and fresh basil to pot. Bring up to temperature then set burner to simmering temp (usually low as you can go). Cover.
  8. Slow simmer sauce with lid on for 4-6 hours, stirring occasionally.
  9. At 4-6 hour mark, take out meat and shred/crush. Add back to pot, stir, and simmer for 30 min to 1 hour.
