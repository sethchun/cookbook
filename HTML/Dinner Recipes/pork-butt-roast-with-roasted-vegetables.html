Pork Butt Roast with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 3 - 4 lbs. Boneless Pork Butt Roast
  • 1 Bunch of Carrots
  • 1 Celery Bunch
  • 1 Onion
  • Herbs/Seasonings:
    • 2 Tsp. Kosher Salt
    • 1 Tsp. Pepper
    • 1 Tsp. Onion Powder
    • 1 Tsp. Garlic Powder
    • 1 Tsp. Smoked Paprika
    • 1 Tsp. Ground Mustard
    • 1 Tbsp. Brown Sugar
    • Several sprigs of fresh Thyme

Instructions:

Meat Prep

  1. Begin by mixing all dry seasonings together.
  2. Massage dry rub into all sides of the pork butt, ensuring even coating.
