Pork Butt Roast with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 3 - 4 lbs. Boneless Pork Butt Roast
  • 1 Bunch of Carrots
  • 1 Celery Bunch
  • 1 Onion
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 4 Tbsp. Butter
  • Herbs/Seasonings:
    • 2 Tsp. Kosher Salt
    • 1 Tsp. Pepper
    • 1 Tsp. Onion Powder
    • 1 Tsp. Garlic Powder
    • 1 Tsp. Smoked Paprika
    • 1 Tsp. Ground Mustard
    • 1 Tbsp. Brown Sugar
    • Several sprigs of fresh Thyme

Instructions:

Meat Prep

  1. Begin by mixing all dry seasonings together.
  2. Massage dry rub into all sides of the pork butt, ensuring even coating.
  3. Let pork butt marinate overnight in fridge.

Roasting Pork:

  1. Take pork out of fridge and preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Place pork on rack in roasting pan, roast for 3-4 hours depending on weight of pork butt.
  3. Once internal temp reaches 185 degrees, remove pork and let rest for 15 to 20 min.

Roasting Veggies:

  1. Cut carrots, celery, and onion into larger chunks (I like 1-1.5 in pieces). Dice fresh Thyme.
  2. Place veggies in bowl with oil, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly.
  3. About an hour before the pork is done, toss veggies into roasting pan.
  4. Once pork is removed, mix butter into veggies and let roast for 15-20 more min.
